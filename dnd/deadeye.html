Deadeye Subclass Features

A Deadeye must have incredible patience for each shot to ensure it counts. With a ranged weapon, the Deadeye has no room for mistakes, guarantees that every attack is precise, and gives no chance for a wasted bullet. View the Deadeye Subclass Table below for a list of features available to this Fighter subclass.

Aiming

3rd Level Deadeye Feature


Choose one target you can see within 300 feet of you as a bonus action. After using this feature, you gain the following benefits until the end of your next turn:

  • Deadly Accurate. When using ranged weapons, you gain bonus damage to damage rolls against the target. The bonus equals half your Fighter level.
  • Precision. Your first ranged weapon attack against the target is made at an advantage.
  • Tracking Shot. You may use a ranged weapon to make an Opportunity Attack against the target if it moves five or more feet or makes a melee attack within your weapon's normal range.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus. You regain all expended uses after you take a long rest.
